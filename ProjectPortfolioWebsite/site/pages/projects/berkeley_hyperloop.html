Berkeley Hyperloop (bLoop)

We are a team from the University of California, Berkeley participating in SpaceX's Hyperloop Competition. We are among the 22 teams selected from the Design Weekend in January 2016 to go onto the Competition Weekend this coming January. We are working on building a 60% scale working model of our Hyperloop pod in order to contribute to the development of a fully functioning Hyperloop.
